Common Rules
Priority — Active player gets priority first each step/phase. Both players must pass for anything to resolve.
State-Based Actions — Checked constantly. Creatures at 0 toughness die, legendary rule, etc.
Stack — Spells and abilities resolve last-in, first-out. Respond before resolution.
Haste — Can attack and use tap abilities the turn it enters.
Summoning Sickness — Creatures that entered this turn can't attack or tap (unless they have Haste).
Deathtouch — Any damage dealt by this creature destroys the damaged creature.
Lifelink — Damage dealt gains you that much life.
Trample — Excess combat damage is dealt to defending player.
First Strike — Deals combat damage before normal creatures.
Double Strike — Deals both first-strike and normal combat damage.
